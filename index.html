About soffyn

I built the app I couldn't find.

Hi, I'm Akia — a neurodivergent Black woman, developer, and accessibility consultant who got tired of wellness apps that weren't built for brains like mine.

I've tried almost every wellness app on the market. They were either too cookie cutter, too demanding, or just too much for my brain. I'm a forgetful girl. Out of sight, out of mind. Streaks don't work for me. My hopes and dreams have a habit of becoming hoarded thoughts in the notes app.

And no app understood the specific exhaustion of spending all day masking — at work, in every room — and then coming home to a wellness app that asked me to perform even more.

I needed something that meets me in the middle. A place to be myself that's gentle, rewarding, and completely okay if I don't show up every single day.

Always there when you need it. Never mad when you don't.

That's soffyn. I built it for me. And I built it for you.
