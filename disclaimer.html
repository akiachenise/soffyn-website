Wellness Disclaimer.

soffyn is a space for your wellbeing — not a medical or mental health service. Here's what that means.

Effective Date: March 1, 2026 | Developed By Akia, LLC

1. soffyn is a wellness app, not a medical or mental health service

soffyn is designed to support your general well-being through affirmations, journaling, goal tracking, and mindfulness features. We genuinely care about your health — and because we do, we want to be clear about what soffyn is and isn't.

2. What soffyn is

  • A personal wellness companion for daily reflection, affirmations, and gentle habit building
  • A supportive space designed specifically for neurodivergent minds
  • A tool built with care, by someone who needed it herself

3. What soffyn is not

  • A licensed mental health service or therapy platform
  • A substitute for professional medical or psychological care
  • A crisis intervention service
  • A diagnostic tool of any kind

The content in soffyn — including affirmations, journaling prompts, and wellness tips — is for informational and inspirational purposes only. It is not medical advice and should not be treated as such.

4. Please reach out if you need support

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, thoughts of self-harm, or any other urgent mental health concern, please reach out to a qualified professional or one of the following resources:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 (US)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • NAMI Helpline: 1-800-950-6264
  • Emergency services: 911

You deserve real support, and these services are here for you.

5. A note from Akia

I built soffyn because I needed it — a gentle, forgiving space that met me where I was, without pressure or judgment. soffyn is an act of care, not a clinical service. I'm a developer and accessibility consultant, not a therapist. Please use soffyn as one part of your wellness toolkit, alongside the other support systems in your life.

Questions? hello@soffyn.com

6. Consent to use

By using soffyn, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this disclaimer and agree that soffyn is not a substitute for professional mental health care.
