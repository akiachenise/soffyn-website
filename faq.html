Everything you want to know about soffyn — and a few things you didn't know you needed to ask.

What is soffyn?

soffyn is a wellness app I built for neurodivergent Black women — and for everyone who needed something like this to exist. Daily affirmations, flexible journaling, goal tracking, a focus timer, quick-capture, and privacy controls. No streaks. No shame. Just a space that meets you where you are.

Who is soffyn for?

soffyn is built specifically for neurodivergent Black women — people with ADHD, autism, anxiety, dyslexia, sensory processing differences, or any brain that doesn't fit the mold. Everyone is welcome. But I center the people who have been most underserved by wellness apps — because that's who I built it for first.

When does soffyn launch?

soffyn is launching September 2026. Join the waitlist to get early access when we open the doors.

What platforms will soffyn be on?

iOS and Android apps are launching September 2026. A web version is available now at soffyn.com.

Will soffyn be free?

If you join the waitlist before launch, yes — you're a Founding Member and soffyn is free for you, forever. Everyone who joins after launch uses soffyn Set at $8.99/month or $64.99/year.

How is soffyn different from Calm or Headspace?

Calm and Headspace are built for neurotypical users. They assume you'll show up consistently, meditate on a schedule, and maintain streaks. soffyn is built for brains that don't work that way. No streaks that punish you for being human. No overwhelming menus. No toxic positivity. Just tools that actually get how your brain works.

Is my data private?

Yes. We respect your privacy. You'll always know what data we collect and why. We never sell your personal information. Read our full Privacy Policy.

What happens after I join the waitlist?

Who built soffyn?

soffyn was built by Akia — a neurodivergent Black woman, developer, and accessibility consultant who couldn't find a wellness app built for a brain like hers. So she built it. soffyn is a product of Developed By Akia, LLC.

Will there be a community?

Is soffyn only for Black women?

soffyn is built specifically for neurodivergent Black women — this community is at the center of every design decision, every affirmation, every feature. Everyone is welcome to use soffyn. But the experience is shaped by and for that community, and we don't apologize for that specificity.
