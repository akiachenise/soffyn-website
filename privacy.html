Last updated: February 2026

We keep this simple because we mean it: your data is yours. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and what we do with it.

Who we are

soffyn is a product of Developed By Akia, LLC. If you have any questions about this policy, email us at akia@soffyn.com.

What we collect

When you join the soffyn waitlist, we collect your first name and email address. That's it. We don't collect your location, phone number, or any other personal information unless you choose to share it with us directly.

Why we collect it

We use your email address to send you updates about soffyn's launch and to invite you to download the app as a founding member when we enter beta. We will never use your email for anything else without your permission.

Who we share it with

We use Mailchimp to manage our waitlist. Your information is stored securely in Mailchimp. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone else. Ever.

How long we keep it

We keep your email on our waitlist until you unsubscribe. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. One click and you're off the list — no questions asked.

Your rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time. Just email us at akia@soffyn.com and we'll take care of it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy we'll update the date at the top and let our waitlist know. We won't make changes that compromise your privacy without telling you first.

Questions?

Email us at akia@soffyn.com. We're real people and we actually respond.
